About Our Company

Pioneering Energy Solutions Across Continents

German-Jo Oil Guru Nigeria Limited is a forward-thinking energy company headquartered in the oil-rich region of Delta State, Nigeria. We provide premium petroleum trading, logistics, consultancy, and comprehensive energy solutions to clients across Africa and global markets.

With a steadfast commitment to excellence, innovation, safety, and sustainable development, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for multinational corporations, government agencies, and energy stakeholders worldwide.
